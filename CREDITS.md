# Credits & attribution — Fish Fillets 4ever

*Fish Fillets 4ever* is a faithful web reimplementation of the 1998 puzzle game
**Fish Fillets**. It stands on the work of several projects and authors.

## Original game

- **Fish Fillets** (1998) — created by **ALTAR interactive** (formerly ALTAR games).
  The gameplay, level design, artwork, music, voices, and story are theirs.

## Game data

- **Fish Fillets NG** ([fillets-ng](https://fillets-ng.sourceforge.net/)) — the
  open-source remake by Ivo Danihelka and contributors. The room data shipped with
  this port is derived from the **GPL-licensed fillets-ng data**. Attribution and the
  data's license terms follow that project.

## Fonts

- **Mulish**, **Manrope**, **Jost** — bundled variable subtitle fonts, each under the
  **SIL Open Font License 1.1**. The license text for each is in
  [`public/fonts/`](public/fonts/) (`OFL-Mulish.txt`, `OFL-Manrope.txt`, `OFL-Jost.txt`).
- **GNU FreeFont / FreeSans** — the enhanced-art subtitle face, under the
  **GNU General Public License** (part of the [GNU FreeFont](https://www.gnu.org/software/freefont/)
  project).
